What is next?

In the past, BibleTime was released "as the Spirit so moves us". At the conclusion of this Bug-a-thon we can provide technical feedback for the direction of the project, and incorporate it into our Development Plan We should also prayerfully consider options, target goals and future direction of the application.

Please, come join us. If you have other issues/requests feel free to show up on IRC or email us at bt-devel@crosswire.org
